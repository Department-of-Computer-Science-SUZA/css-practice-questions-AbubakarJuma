Coffee, derived from the roasted seeds of the Coffea plant, is a beloved beverage enjoyed by millions worldwide. Here's a detailed look. The coffee industry is a major global economic force, encompassing cultivation, processing, distribution, and consumption. Coffee-producing countries, primarily located in regions such as Latin America, Africa, and Asia, rely heavily on coffee production for economic growth and livelihoods. Additionally, the global coffee market is influenced by factors such as supply and demand dynamics, weather conditions, and market trends.
